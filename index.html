III

At the Table

+Rules to keep straight
  • Casting Snapdax normally does nothing special — the 4 / 4 only fires when he mutates.
  • You need not recast Snapdax to retrigger — mutating any cheap creature onto the pile re-fires it.
  • Copies take only the top card and do not mutate; Helm, Jaxis, and Saw tokens will not re-fire the trigger. Use them as bodies and fresh mutate bases.
  • Never Saw in Half your own Snapdax pile — it is destroyed, and you copy only a small, legendary top card. Saw non-legendary payoffs instead.
  • Command Beacon returns Snapdax to hand for a tax-free recast.
  • Mutate onto non-Human creatures you own. Slot new cards under to keep Snapdax on top.
+Lines that win the game
  • ComboBloodthirsty Conqueror + Vito — any life loss loops, the table dies.
  • ComboConqueror + Marauding / Cliffhaven — any lifegain loops, draining all opponents at once.
  • BlowoutRepercussion + Blasphemous Act — mass damage redirected to faces; Barbed Servitor survives it.
  • BeatdownMjölnir + Solphim on a double-strike pile — mutate for 16, then swing with lifelink drain.
+Tutor priority
  • Drainer + lifegain online → Bloodthirsty Conqueror for the win.
  • Already holding Conqueror → Vito, the cleanest closer.
  • Turn 1–2 → Sol Ring. Low on gas → Black Market Connections.
  • Pile assembled → Solphim, Mjölnir, or Roaming Throne.
  • Threat plus open removal → Swiftfoot Boots or Skrelv.
  • Cast Vampiric Tutor on the end step before your turn to blunt its cost.
+Mulligan
  • Keep two or more lands — ideally a white source for {W}{W} — plus a rock or cheap base.
  • Ideal: an early mutate base (Mysterious Egg, Boot Nipper, Skrelv) beside a draw engine.
  • Ship hands with no white source, all five-drops, or no early play.
  • Snapdax need not be in hand — he waits in the command zone.
+Turn sequencing
IRamp & bases. Rocks, Land Tax, a cheap non-Human. Avoid over-committing into open mana.
IIEngine. Land a draw engine or a drain payoff such as Vito or Marauding.
IIIFirst mutate. Only with protection or clear tempo. Bait removal against loaded tables.
IVDoubler. Add Solphim, Mjölnir, or Roaming Throne, then chain mutates.
VClose. Combo drain, Repercussion plus a wipe, or a lifelinking double-striker and Vito the difference.
